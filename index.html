when did panipuri invented?

Two things play an important role in completing Golgappa, potatoes and water. Without them, this dish is completely tasteless and incomplete. Since both these ingredients came to India about 300-400 years ago, historians believe that the dish was originated in Magadha, which is present-day South Bihar. (source: reference from google)
